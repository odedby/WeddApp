WeddApp


Welcome to WeddApp!

This application is for Hagit and Oded's wedding guests.

We would like to welcome you to download the app and give the wedding additional flavour.

The application offers different views:

1. Photo sharing (updates in real time). That way, each photo you take using the app can be uploaded to a special picasa album.

2. Map - To navigate to the wedding location

3. Invitaion - A copy of the invitation

4. RSVP - Approve your participation (that way we wonn't trouble you with calls)

5. Table - Find out what is your table number (available at the day of the wedding only)

6. Greeting - Send the lovely couple a greeting using the app

7. Credits - If you enjoyd one of our wonderful service providers, you can get to their website from the app


For any comments and/or suggestion, feel free to call us or send a mail to hagit.oded@gmail.com

Note that the iPhone version a bit more tricky to download. explnations in the iPhone section

No personal information will be used or even captured


Thank you, and we hope to see you soon at our wedding!

Hagit and Oded
